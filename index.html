The 16th Annual Conference of the North American Chapter of the Association for Computational Linguistics: Human Language Technologies will be held in New Orleans, June 1 to June 6, 2018 at the Hyatt Regency New Orleans hotel.

NEW To acknowledge the prevalence and impact of language technologies in real-word applications, NAACL is introducing an Industry track that is targeted to the dissemination of technological inventions, system implementations and challenges in speech and natural language processing based industry-scale applications.
Paper Submission Deadline: Feb 20, 2018.) See Call for Paper for more details.
NEW NAACL will have on-site childcare offered by Kiddie Corp for six days. Childcare for student NAACL members will be at a reduced price.
